Since we were together in class.

Batch 2081

Batch 2081 of Kinder Garden Academy was more than just a group of students. We were a family who learned, laughed and grew together. Every classroom moment, every joke, every challenge created unforgettable memories. Though life will take us in different directions, the bond we built will always remain. Our school days will forever live in our hearts.

Iconic Memories

Tour Experience

Our school tour was one of the most exciting experiences of class 10. Traveling together, sharing food and capturing photos created unforgettable moments. The journey was full of laughter and adventure. That tour strengthened our friendship and gave us memories we will never forget. Those moments will stay with us forever.

Hostel Life

Hostel life during class 10 taught us independence and responsibility. We studied together, shared late-night talks and supported each other. Sometimes it was difficult but those struggles created strong friendships. Those nights of preparation, jokes and dreams made our bond stronger. Hostel life became a memory we will always cherish.

SEE Examination

The SEE exam was one of the most important milestones in our school life. It represented years of learning and preparation. During that time we encouraged each other and faced the challenge together. Those exam days were stressful but also meaningful. Completing SEE together made us proud and confident for the future.
